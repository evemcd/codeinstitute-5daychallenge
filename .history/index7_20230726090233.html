How to make Tea

image

At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart

ingredients

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Plant based(Optional)
  • Sugar/Honey (Optional)

Preparation

  1. Boil the water
  2. Pour the water into your favourite mug
  3. Dunk the tea bag into the water
  4. Let it rest for the required time - check the packaging
  5. Remove the tea bag with the string provided or a spoon
  6. Add milk and/or sweetener to your liking
  7. Enoy!
